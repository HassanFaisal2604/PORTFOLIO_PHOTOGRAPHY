About Me

With a background in Computer Science, I bring a unique technical perspective to photography, understanding the inner workings of digital imaging, image processing, and color science. My analytical eye and attention to detail allow me to capture high-quality images while quickly identifying and solving technical issues.

I have experience shooting in diverse conditions, from bustling markets to low-light indoor environments, and I'm also interested in mobile photography, exploring how smartphones are changing the landscape of photography. While my portfolio features DSLR shots, I embrace the creative possibilities mobile devices offer.
